Crystal Collector

The Rules

  • The game will display a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals displayed below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score is greater than the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts the game will change the values of each crystal.
